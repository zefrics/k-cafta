Frequently Asked Questions

Q.How can I access market reports and course content on the K-CA FTA Internationalization Platform?

The market insight reports and course contents can be accessed by members of the online platform at free of charge. Once you your registry is authorized, please log in and click on the course you’re interested to access the contents.

Q.How much does it cost to register on the K-CA FTA Internationalization Platform?

Registering on the K-CA FTA Internationalization Platform and using all its features and services is completely free.

Q.How can I modify the information on my personal/company profile?

You can change your user id, email address, password, company information on Mypage after login.
