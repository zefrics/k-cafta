Korea Import Fair 2025

07-07-2025 ~ 09-07-2025
Republic of Korea
  • Other

The Korea Import Fair is intended to help foreign manufacturers and suppliers by providing an opportunity to introduce and advertise their famous brands and quality products to Korean importers and consumers. KIF is hosted by Korea Importers Association(KOIMA) which is a private organization with over 50 years of experience in the promotion of imports into Korea. The event is also sponsored by the Ministry of Trade, Industry & Energy(MOTIE), Korea Trade-Investment Promotion Agency (KOTRA), as well as many foreign embassies in Korea. In 2023, a total of 87 companies from 47 countries participated. From the Latin American region, Guatemala, Mexico, Costa Rica, Peru, Ecuador, and Bolivia participated in the event. As for the type of items exhibited, this event is a comprehensive expo exhibiting a variety of foreign products. Thus, products from raw materials to intermediate goods or final high-tech goods can be introduced to Korean importers. Aside from the exhibition, the fair also provides B2B 1:1 meetings and Korea’s import policy session.

Korea International Livestock EXPO (KISTOCK) 2025

00-00-2025 ~ 00-00-2025
Republic of Korea
  • Prepared Foodstuffs

KISTOCK is a sole livestock professional fair in Korea, which is co-hosted by Ministry of Agricultures Food and Rural Affairs and 6 Producer Organization including Korea Poultry Association, Korea Pork Producers Association and more. The fair enables livestock farmers and enterprises exchange information and promote their businesses. As one of the major displaying items is feed sector, KISTOCK offers various business opportunities to the project’s pre-selected item Livestock Feeding (230990). Holding biannually since 1999, the 12th KISTOCK held in 2023 mobilized 214 companies with 665 booths from 10 different countries and 26,053 visitors. Although the fair’s main events are specialized in knowledge sharing with around 20 academic seminars and conferences, the fair gathers producers and buyers from entire livestock industry. The latest show in 2023 newly attempted hybrid type of fair by adopting IT technology driven exhibition method such as Digital Showroom and Onsite Live. As the scale of previous performance of the fair before pandemic was much larger, it is expected to attract more visitors as well as international business opportunities.

CABEI-Korea Business Forum 2024

11-11-2024 ~ 12-11-2024
Costa Rica
  • Other

Korea-Latin America Business Forum aims to strengthen business ties between Korea and LAC countries. It is jointly organized by the Korean Council on Latin America & the Caribbean, Korea Institute for International Economic Policy, Korea International Trade Association, Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy of Korea, and International Construction Association of Korea. The dates for the event in 2024 have not been announced. Yet, the event has been held annually in November. The event will most likely be held in Seoul, Korea. Throughout the event, simultaneous interpretation to Korean, English, and Spanish will be available. The event not only provides useful insights into the future challenges and opportunities to expand and strengthen economic and cooperation ties between both regions, but also provides 1:1 consulting with certified customs broker and patent attorney. The participants can apply for the consulting services via online prior to the event.

  • Animal & Vegetable Products

Korea Metal Week 2024

16-10-2024 ~ 18-10-2024
Republic of Korea
  • Metal & Mineral Products

KOREA METAL WEEK is most important trade fair for the metal industry. It is globally recognized marketplace for more than 350 exhibitors and 17,000 experts engaged in industry, research and development, and all aspects of production - presenting everything from raw materials, semi-finished and finished products to machinery equipment. It is the first industrial exhibition in Korea that was held without change to the promised schedule through 2020 and 2021 during the global pandemic COVID-19. In 2024, the exhibition aims to serve as a starting point for revitalizing the domestic market and advancing into overseas markets. The product spectrum is rounded off by 14 sectors, Fastener & Wire, Foundry & Die Casting, Automobile & Machinery Parts, Press & Forging, Tube & Pipe, Metal Surface Treatment & Painting, Aluminum, Laser & Welding, Fundamental Manufacturing Technology, Pump Technology, Tools, etc. Among the products, the project’s pre-selected item : Waste and scrap of aluminum (760200), Copper (260300), Nickel (260400), Zinc(260800) are included.
