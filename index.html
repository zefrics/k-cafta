About Platform

The Platform aims to bolster trade between Republic of Korea and Central America as a centralized hub for trade related information and capacity building contents.

This platform addresses information asymmetry and enhances local stakeholders’ access to ROK market information.

This platform will contribute to the diversification and expansion of CA economy, as well as strengthening of partnerships with ROK businesses.

Sponsored by

The Republic of Korea(ROK) joined CABEI in 2020. The Ministry of Economy and Finance (MOEF) created the Korea-CABEI Partnership Single Donor Trust Fund (KTF) for identification and preparation of projects. The creation of this platform was fully funded by KTF.

Created by

The Central American Bank for Economic Integration(CABEI) is a multilateral development bank financing projects that foster development of CA countries and strengthen regional integration.

Operated by

The Chamber of Commerce Korea-LAC(CAMCOR) is the operator of this platform. With expertise on trade facilitation, business advisory, CAMCOR sustains platform service and contents.
