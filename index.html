Sports Diagnosis Program é

Improving starts with objectifying yourself. This program allows athlete and coaches to move forward together in a better direction. Figure skating is a demanding sport that requires skill, artistry, and program composition strategy all within a limited amount of time. Owl Sports helps athletes achieve self-objectification by diagnosing their current programs and physical condition based on rules and scoring methods that are in line with trends.

Experts in each field

A group of experts who understand the characteristics of the sports and the latest trends.

Based on precision measurement and data

Objective monitoring of the athlete's current status with quantification of measurable areas.

Report

Provides analysis reports with expert opinions to help determine their future direction.

Technical Diagnosis - for Figure skating

It is important to create a complete program by strengthening strengths and complementing weaknesses. It is necessary to structure the program so that the athlete can achieve the maximum performance at his/her level. Receive an assessment of the current program structure and skills through an assessment that reflects the latest scoring trends and guides the athlete in accordance with the current ISU standard trends.

Technical Elements

Diagnose whether the skills corresponding to the technical sector are being used well. In particular, check whether the skills appropriate to the program are organized to match the player's level check whether the player is digesting them well and drawing out their strengths, etc.

Program Components

Experts such as the judge and technical panel will assess the overall program composition and scoring strategy.

Physical Performance

Based on a healthy body, you can continuously improve your exercise ability. Accurately identify your current condition through basic exercise ability and body structure diagnosis. Provide solutions for improving problems.

Basic Examination

Using various medical measurement equipment, measure basic exercise performance abilities such as muscle strength, muscle endurance, and lung capacity, and identify areas that need strengthening based on numerical data.

Body Structure Diagnosis

Diagnose the player's current body based on objective data. Recognize that physical structural defects may be caused by posture or habits, etc., and use this as basis for supplementation

Establishment of Correction Strategy

Compile diagnostic data to provide solutions for improving your body and physical skills. Suggest massage, exercise, and eating habits for correction and help you train efficiently

Custom Item Creation

Discover, produce, and provide customized items exclusively for athletes to improve performance and basic abilities. For example, you can improve foot condition and athletic performance through customized insoles.
